# Day 1 - Introduction to Cybersecurity

## What is cybersecurity?
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from cyber attacks.

## Why is it important?
It helps protect personal information, financial data, and systems from being stolen or damaged.

## Example of cyber threats
- Phishing
- Malware
- Ransomware

## What I learned today
Today I learned the basics of cybersecurity and why it is important in our daily lives.
